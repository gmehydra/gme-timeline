GameStop Company Ownership
As of {{ lastUpdated }}

As a publicly traded company, GameStop is unique because a significant portion of all issued shares are owned by registered shareholders.

Held by registered holders at Computershare

All shares held with GameStop's transfer agent Computershare are considered "registered" which provides title ownership of shares, but of those shares there is a distinction between DRS shares and DSPP shares with implications on possession.

Held by Cede & Co on behalf of the DTCC

All shares not held by registered holders are held by Cede & Co on behalf of the DTCC. This includes all shares held in brokerage accounts by retail investors, insiders, institutions, mutual funds, ETFs, and any other types of beneficial ownership.
